Course Modules

Module 1: Introduction to Locksmithing

Duration: 2 weeks

  • History of locksmithing
  • Ethics and legal regulations
  • Overview of locksmith tools
  • Safety protocols
  • Types of locks and terminology
  • Professionalism and customer service

Module 2: Residential Locks & Security

Duration: 3 weeks

  • Pin tumbler locks, wafer locks, deadbolts
  • Key duplication and key impressioning
  • Lock repair and maintenance
  • Installing residential security systems
  • Emergency lockout procedures

Module 3: Commercial Locks & Advanced Mechanisms

Duration: 4 weeks

  • High-security locks and master key systems
  • Access control systems and electronic locks
  • Safe locks and vaults
  • Lock picking and bypass techniques (legal)

Module 4: Automotive Locksmithing

Duration: 3 weeks

  • Car locks and ignition systems
  • Transponder keys and key programming
  • Emergency car lockout procedures
  • Advanced diagnostic tools

Module 5: Locksmith Business & Certification

Duration: 2 weeks

  • Business setup and marketing for locksmiths
  • Customer service best practices
  • State licensing and national certification preparation
  • Professional ethics and ongoing education
